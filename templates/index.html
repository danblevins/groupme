A complete guide to your GroupMe friends' superlatives

This is a super simple application for analyzing your GroupMe friend group and awarding annual superlatives. This app is built using Flask with template inheritance, Spacy, Pandas/NumPy, and Seaborn.

Superlatives

The available superlatives are:

  • 🕶Most Popular
  • 🗣Most Talkative
  • 📖Most Verbose
  • 📱Most Social
  • 🤷‍♂️Most Nicknames
  • ✊Most Supportive

Examples

Go to superlatives, time series NLP analysis, textual NLP analysis

Documentation

Simply visit https://web.groupme.com/profile/export, click "Create Export", choose "Message Data", and select the group.

Once the file is exported, download and unzip it, upload the message.json file to this site, and hit the submit button.
